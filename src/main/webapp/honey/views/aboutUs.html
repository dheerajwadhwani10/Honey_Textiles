About Company

Tuni Textile’s is traditionally recognized for its excellence in quality, product/design range, superior customer service and reliability. The company produces over ~7.2 million meters per annum on latest European looms. Apart from manufacturing synthetic shirting, the firm is also engaged in trading actives and job work. The company was incorporated in 1987. With almost 3 decades of experience under its belt, we are in a position to provide exceptional quality, technical know-how and various design and product innovations to our customers. Our manufacturing facility houses the best technology that grey-fabric manufacturing has to offer. Our core strength is in new product/design development and a hassle free quality promise. We have a systematic quality culture right from all in-ward materials, on-loom inspection, grey-fabric checking and superior mending facilities.
